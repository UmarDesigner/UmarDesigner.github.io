Sisterhood of Healing Inc - Where transformation begins

No doubt, 2021 was a roller coaster for all of us.

 

A combination of the pandemic, covid, new variants, quarantine, financial efforts, and whatnot.

 

As it is leaving, also leaving some scars behind.

 

Scars that run deep down into our hearts.

Let me ask you a question.

 

How does a doctor treat an infected wound? 

 

Most likely, he starts by using a scalpel to open it up, clean it, and then asks us to keep the wound clean while it heals.

 

When it comes to healing our emotional wounds, the process isn’t much different.

 

In this case, the scalpel is TRUTH.

 

👉 We can use it to open our wounds and reveal lies.

 

👉 Then we clean out the poison with forgiveness.

 

👉 Finally, we can keep the wound clean until it’s fully healed—using love.

 

See, the world is full of lies and illusions, which can make the truth pretty difficult to come by.

 

Forgiveness isn’t easy, but it’s necessary. You need to extend it to everyone who has ever hurt you.

 

Not because that person needs to be forgiven, but because YOU don’t deserve to continue suffering from the memory of the things that person did.
 

Again, it’s not easy but it's worth your mental energies.

Big Love 💌
